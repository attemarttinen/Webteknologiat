League of Legends

What is LoL Challenger? Challenger is the highest rank in League of Legends and represents the top 300 or 200 players of each region. To achieve Challenger rank, players will have to make it through the eight previous LoL tiers.

Counter Strike Global Offensive

The Global Elite rank in competitive CS:GO is the light at the end of the ranked tunnel. Global Elite players epitomize the top one percent of CS:GO players in their respective regions and represent the best of the best. It's no easy task to go all the way from the lowly Silver ranks up to Global Elite.
